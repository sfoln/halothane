Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

SFOLN LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you buy or use Halothane, and what we do with it. The short version: we collect as little as possible, and we don't sell your data.

The Halothane app

Halothane runs entirely on your Mac. It does not send your process data, memory usage, or any personal information off your device. It has no telemetry and no analytics, and it does not require an account to run.

Buying Halothane

When you purchase, payment is handled by Stripe, which acts as the merchant of record. Stripe collects the information needed to process your payment (such as your email and card details) under its own privacy policy. We receive your email address and order details so we can deliver your download link and license key and provide support. We do not receive or store your full card number.

Our website

halothane.app uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-first and cookieless — it does not track you across sites or build a profile of you. We use it only to see aggregate page views.

How we use your information

  • To deliver your purchase (download link and license key).
  • To provide customer support and process refunds.
  • To meet legal and tax obligations related to your purchase.

We do not sell your personal information or share it for advertising.

Your choices

You may request a copy or deletion of the personal information we hold about you (your email and order records) by contacting us. We keep purchase records as required for tax and accounting.
